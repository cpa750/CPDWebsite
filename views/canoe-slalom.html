About Canoe Slalom


Canoe slalom is a highly competitive, adrenaline-pumping sport that involves racing through a set course in a whitewater river. A single mistake can prove devastating, with the tenths of seconds deciding the medals. Top athletes put in thousands of hours of training year-round in all sorts of conditions, hoping to become the best.
